Notes:
Originally composed for Roland MT-32. The AdLib
conversion is mostly servicable, except for the
ambient tracks (e.g. 44/52/53). Almost all of
the music is original, even as it uses John
Williams' leitmotifs.

In Tracks 30/39/45/58/75/118/119/120, the game
enables and disables MIDI channels to reflect
game events. See the Notes of each file to
learn what events are being reflected.

Track 32 is unused in the game and replaced by
a repeat of Track 29.

Track 63 consists of four files playing at the
same time which have different loop points that
never sync (or at least don't after playing for
two hours). The VGM pack version loops after
one full iteration of the longest of the four
files.

Track 151 is Track 136 with MIDI channels 6 and
7 enabled. These channels are normally enabled
only with the Roland MT-32 sound device
selected.

Original sound driver: iMuse (ADLIB.IMS)
